# Loom Demo Script — 60 seconds

## Setup
- Browser open to recrutas.ai, logged in as candidate with NO resume uploaded yet
- Have a sample resume PDF ready on desktop
- Screen recording, face cam in corner

## Script

**[0-5s] Hook**
"I built a free tool that matches you to jobs by actual skills — not keywords — and auto-applies for you. Here's 60 seconds."

**[5-15s] Upload Resume**
> Action: Click Upload Resume, drag PDF, show processing animation
"Upload your resume. AI extracts your skills, experience, and what you're actually good at. No forms."

**[15-30s] See Matches**
> Action: Show job feed with match scores, scroll through 3-4 jobs
"You get matched to real jobs from company career pages — not ghost listings. Each one has a score and tells you WHY you're a fit."

**[30-45s] Agent Apply**
> Action: Click Apply on a high-match job, show agent apply starting
"Click Apply. An AI agent fills out the entire application on the company's site and submits it for you. Name, email, work history, screening questions — everything."

**[45-55s] Results**
> Action: Show application tracker with submitted status
"No more copy-pasting into 200 forms. No more applying to jobs that closed 3 months ago. 14,000 active jobs, refreshed daily."

**[55-60s] CTA**
"It's free. No paywall. Link below."

## Tips
- Keep energy high but natural — you're showing a friend, not pitching VCs
- Don't explain how it works technically — just show results
- If agent apply takes too long to demo live, cut to the confirmation email
