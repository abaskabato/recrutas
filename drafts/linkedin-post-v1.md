# LinkedIn Post Draft

I'm a software engineer at Amazon. On nights and weekends, I built Recrutas — a free job matching platform for developers.

Here's the problem: people spend 3-6 months job searching, mass-applying to hundreds of roles, and getting ghosted by 95% of companies.

Here's what Recrutas does differently:

1. Upload your resume (PDF). That's it.
2. AI matches you to 14,000+ real jobs by actual skill fit — not keyword spam. Each match tells you WHY you're competitive.
3. Click "Apply" and an AI agent fills out the entire application on the company's site and submits it for you.

No ghost jobs. Jobs are pulled directly from company career pages and refreshed daily.

No paywall. Free, always.
